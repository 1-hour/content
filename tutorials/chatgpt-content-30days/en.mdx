## 📋 Prerequisites

- ChatGPT account (free version works fine)
- Google Sheets or similar spreadsheet tool
- Your niche/topic focus chosen (e.g., productivity, fitness, career advice)
- Basic understanding of your target audience

## Step 1: Build Your Hook Formula Arsenal (0–15 min)

First, create a document with proven hook templates. These formulas work because they trigger curiosity, controversy, or immediate value.

Copy these 8 viral hook formulas:

```
Formula 1 - The Mistake: "I [spent money/time] on [topic]. Here's what I learned"
Formula 2 - The Controversy: "Why [popular belief] is actually [opposite]"
Formula 3 - The List: "[Number] [tools/tips/secrets] that [specific benefit]"
Formula 4 - The Story: "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe] by [method]"
Formula 5 - The Warning: "Stop [common behavior] before it [negative consequence]"
Formula 6 - The Hack: "[Number] [platform] [hacks/tricks] they don't want you to know"
Formula 7 - The Comparison: "[Option A] vs [Option B]: Which one actually [delivers result]"
Formula 8 - The Behind Scenes: "What nobody tells you about [topic/industry]"
```

Now tell ChatGPT your niche. Use this prompt:

```
My niche is [YOUR NICHE - e.g., "remote work productivity for beginners"]. 
My audience struggles with [MAIN PAIN POINT - e.g., "staying focused while working from home"].

Generate 3 hook examples for each of these 8 formulas, tailored to my niche:
[paste your 8 formulas here]

Make each hook under 15 words and immediately clear about the value.
```

<Answer>Yes, each hook clearly states the value and fits my audience's pain points</Answer>
</Checkpoint>

## Step 2: Generate Your 30-Day Content Pipeline (15–30 min)

Create a content mix that keeps your audience engaged. The best creators use this ratio:
- 40% Educational (tutorials, tips, how-tos)
- 30% Inspirational (stories, wins, behind-scenes)
- 20% Controversial (hot takes, myth-busting)
- 10% Promotional (your products/services)

Use this ChatGPT prompt to generate your ideas:

```
Create a 30-day content calendar for [YOUR NICHE]. Follow this ratio:
- 12 educational posts (tutorials, step-by-step guides, tool reviews)
- 9 inspirational posts (success stories, lessons learned, motivation)
- 6 controversial posts (challenge popular beliefs, hot takes)
- 3 promotional posts (showcase expertise, soft sell)

For each post, provide:
1. Post type
2. Main topic
3. Target audience pain point it solves
4. Key takeaway

Format as a numbered list. Make topics specific and actionable.
```

Review the output. Good topics are specific (not "how to be productive" but "how to batch email responses in 15 minutes"). Bad topics are vague or overly broad.

Now refine weak topics with this follow-up:

```
Posts #[X, Y, Z] are too vague. Make them more specific by adding:
- Exact numbers or timeframes
- Specific tools or methods
- Clear before/after outcomes

Rewrite those 3 posts only.
```

<Checkpoint>
Does each of your 30 topics solve a specific problem your audience faces, with measurable outcomes they can achieve?
<Answer>Yes, each topic is specific and actionable with clear value</Answer>
</Checkpoint>

## Step 3: Write Viral Hooks and Full Post Drafts (30–45 min)

Transform your topics into scroll-stopping content. Start with your 5 highest-value topics (usually the educational ones solve urgent problems).

Use this prompt for each post:

```
Write a complete social media post for this topic: [TOPIC FROM YOUR LIST]

Follow this structure:
1. HOOK: Use one of these formulas [paste 2-3 relevant hook formulas from Step 1]
2. PROBLEM: What pain does this solve? (1-2 lines)
3. SOLUTION: The main content (3-5 bullet points or short paragraphs)
4. CALL TO ACTION: Ask a question or request engagement

Optimize for:
- Xiaohongshu: More personal storytelling, use emojis sparingly
- Twitter: Punchy, thread-worthy, include relevant hashtags

Write both versions.
```

Here's what good vs. bad looks like:

**Bad hook:** "Today I want to share some productivity tips"
**Good hook:** "I tried 47 productivity apps in 30 days. Only these 3 actually saved me time"

**Bad content:** Long paragraphs with theory
**Good content:** Numbered steps with specific actions

For your first 5 posts, ask ChatGPT to improve weak hooks:

```
Rate this hook on viral potential (1-10): "[YOUR HOOK]"

If under 8/10, rewrite it using these criteria:
- Creates immediate curiosity or controversy
- Promises specific value (numbers, timeframes)
- Speaks directly to [YOUR AUDIENCE]'s biggest frustration
```

## Step 4: Build Your Content Calendar System (45–55 min)

Create a tracking spreadsheet with these columns:
- Date
- Platform (Twitter/Xiaohongshu/Both)
- Hook
- Post Type (Educational/Inspirational/Controversial/Promotional)
- Content Status (Draft/Ready/Posted)
- Performance Notes

Organize your posting schedule strategically:
- Start each week with educational content (Monday engagement is higher)
- Use controversial takes mid-week when people are most active
- End weeks with inspirational content
- Space promotional posts every 10 days

Use this ChatGPT prompt to optimize your schedule:

```
I have 30 social media posts planned. Help me schedule them across 30 days for maximum engagement.

Rules:
- Post daily
- Start each week with educational content
- Use controversial posts on Tuesday/Wednesday
- Never put 2 promotional posts in the same week
- Balance content types throughout the month

My post types: [list your 30 posts with their types]

Give me a day-by-day schedule with reasoning for timing choices.
```

## Step 5: Ship It (55–60 min)

Create your reusable prompt library so you never run out of content ideas again. Save these 5 prompts:

**Prompt 1 - Idea Generator:**
```
Generate 10 [content type] ideas for [your niche] that solve [specific pain point]. Each idea should be actionable within [timeframe] and include specific numbers or outcomes.
```

**Prompt 2 - Hook Improver:**
```
Rewrite this hook to be more viral: "[hook]". Make it create curiosity, include specific numbers, and speak to [audience]'s main frustration. Give me 3 versions.
```

**Prompt 3 - Content Expander:**
```
Turn this topic "[topic]" into a complete social media post with: viral hook, problem statement, 5 actionable steps, and engagement question. Write for [platform].
```

**Prompt 4 - Trend Capitalizer:**
```
[Trending topic] is popular right now. Create 5 content angles that connect this trend to [your niche] in valuable ways for [your audience].
```

**Prompt 5 - Performance Analyzer:**
```
My post "[hook]" got [engagement numbers]. Analyze why it worked/didn't work and create 3 similar posts that could perform better.
```

Test one prompt now by generating next week's content with Prompt 1.

🎉 You now have 30 days of content planned, drafted, and scheduled, plus a system that generates unlimited ideas on demand.
