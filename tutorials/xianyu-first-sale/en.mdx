## 📋 Prerequisites

- Xianyu app installed with verified account (real-name authentication completed)
- WeChat account for customer service
- Basic digital skills (can create PDFs, organize files)
- One digital asset ready to sell (notes, templates, guides, or mini-course)
- Smartphone with decent camera for product photos

## Step 1: Choose Your Digital Product & Create Compelling Copy (0–12 min)

Pick a digital product you can deliver instantly. Best performers on Xianyu: study notes, design templates, productivity tools, mini-courses, checklists.

Open Xianyu and search these terms to see what's selling:
- "学习资料" (study materials)  
- "模板" (templates)
- "笔记" (notes)
- "课程" (courses)

Notice the pattern: successful listings promise specific outcomes, not generic content.

❌ Bad: "Marketing Notes Collection"
✅ Good: "30天涨粉10万小红书运营笔记"

Write your product title using this formula:
**[Time Promise] + [Specific Result] + [Tool/Platform] + [Content Type]**

Examples:
- "7天学会拍照修图手机摄影教程"
- "100个爆款文案模板新媒体必备"
- "大学英语四级满分笔记整理"

Write your description in 3 parts:
1. Problem (what frustrates your buyer)
2. Solution (what your product delivers)  
3. Proof (results, testimonials, or credentials)

## Step 2: Design Eye-Catching Product Images & Set Up Listing (12–28 min)

Xianyu is visual-first. Your main image determines if people click.

Create your main product image:
1. Use bright, contrasting colors (orange, red, blue work best)
2. Add large, bold text showing your key benefit
3. Include "before/after" elements if possible
4. Add trust signals: "已售XXX份" or "好评率99%"

Take screenshots of your digital content. Show:
- Table of contents/outline
- Sample pages that look valuable
- Any certificates or credentials
- Customer testimonials (if you have them)

In Xianyu app:
1. Tap the "+" button to create new listing
2. Upload 6-9 images (main image + content previews)
3. Select category "数码" → "其他数码配件" 
4. Enter your title and description
5. Set condition as "全新" (new)

Don't publish yet - you'll optimize pricing first.

## Step 3: Price Strategy & Competitor Research (28–42 min)

Research your competition by searching your product category. Check 10-15 similar listings and record:
- Their prices
- Number of sales ("已售" count)
- Main selling points
- Image quality

Price positioning strategy:
- If you're new: Price 20-30% below top competitors
- If you have unique angle: Match middle-tier pricing  
- Always end in .9 (¥19.9, not ¥20)

Sweet spot for digital products on Xianyu: ¥9.9 - ¥39.9

Calculate your target: Find the average price of your top 5 competitors, then subtract 25%.

Example calculation:
- Competitor prices: ¥25, ¥30, ¥28, ¥22, ¥35
- Average: ¥28
- Your price: ¥28 × 0.75 = ¥21 → Set at ¥19.9

Add your price to the listing but don't publish yet.

## Step 4: Set Up Automated Fulfillment System (42–52 min)

Manual delivery kills your time. Set up automation so customers get files instantly.

**Option 1: WeChat Auto-Reply (Recommended for beginners)**
1. Create a WeChat folder with your digital files
2. Upload files to a cloud service (Baidu Pan, WeChat File Helper)
3. Create shareable links with passwords
4. Set up WeChat auto-reply with the download information

**Option 2: QR Code Method**
1. Create a simple webpage or document with download links
2. Generate a QR code pointing to it
3. Include QR code in your product images
4. Customers scan after payment to access files

In your Xianyu listing description, add:
"付款后请加微信：[your WeChat ID] 自动发送资料"

Test your delivery system:
1. Ask a friend to add your WeChat
2. Send them the auto-reply
3. Confirm they can download files successfully
4. Time the process (should be under 2 minutes)

## Step 5: Ship It (52–60 min)

Publish your listing and get your first customer.

Click "发布" to make your listing live. Immediately:

1. **Share strategically**: Post to your WeChat Moments, relevant QQ groups, or social media with caption: "整理了一份[product type]，需要的朋友可以看看"

2. **Engage with similar listings**: Browse competitor products and leave helpful comments. Some users will check your profile and see your products.

3. **Optimize for Xianyu search**: Add relevant hashtags in comments like #学习资料 #模板 #笔记

4. **Set up notifications**: Enable Xianyu push notifications so you respond to inquiries within minutes.

When you get your first message, respond with:
"您好！这份资料包含[specific contents]，付款后立即发送下载链接，有什么问题可以随时问我 😊"
