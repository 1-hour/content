A complete Xiaohongshu presence with:
- Clear niche positioning (fitness beginner, career switcher, budget foodie, etc.)

Example winning note structure:
```
Hook: "3个月从120斤到95斤，我只做了这3件事"
Promise: Share exact daily routine

CTA: "收藏不迷路，明天分享运动清单"
```

## 📋 Prerequisites
- Xiaohongshu app installed on your phone
- Basic photo editing capability (phone camera is enough)
- 30 minutes of personal experience in any hobby/skill/job
- Willingness to share knowledge publicly

## Step 1: Find Your Niche Sweet Spot (0–15 min)

Open Xiaohongshu and search these 5 proven beginner niches. Pick the one where you can answer "yes" to both questions:

**Fitness Journey**: Search "减肥日记" (weight loss diary)
- Can you share before/after progress or daily habits?
- Do you have 3+ workout or meal tips?

**Career Switch**: Search "转行经验" (career change experience)  
- Have you changed jobs/industries in the past 2 years?
- Can you share interview tips or skill-building methods?

**Budget Living**: Search "省钱攻略" (money-saving tips)
- Do you have specific ways to save on food/shopping/living?
- Can you show price comparisons or deals you found?

**Study Hacks**: Search "学习方法" (study methods)
- Are you learning a new skill/language/certification?
- Can you share apps, schedules, or progress tracking?

**Beauty Basics**: Search "平价护肤" (affordable skincare)
- Do you have a simple skincare routine that works?
- Can you review products under 100 RMB?

Write down your chosen niche and 3 specific topics you can cover:

```
My niche: Career Switch to Tech
Topic 1: How I learned coding in 3 months
Topic 2: 5 free resources that got me interviews  
Topic 3: Salary negotiation that increased my offer by 8K
```

</Checkpoint>

## Step 2: Build Profile + Write 3 Hook-Driven Notes (15–35 min)

**Profile Setup (5 minutes)**

Tap your profile icon, then edit:
- **Username**: YourNiche + Your angle (like "省钱小雨" or "转行成功的小李")
- **Bio**: "分享[your niche]真实经验 | 已帮助XXX人 | 私信必回"
- **Avatar**: Clear face photo or simple graphic related to your niche

**Write Note 1: The Number Hook (5 minutes)**

Use this formula: "[Time period] + [Impressive result] + [Simple method]"

Bad example: "我的减肥心得"
Good example: "2个月瘦了15斤，我只改了这3个习惯"

Your turn:
```
Hook: ___个月/天___了___[result]，我只___[method]
Body: List 3-5 specific steps you took
End: 明天分享___，记得关注不迷路
```

**Write Note 2: The Mistake Hook (5 minutes)**

Format: "千万别___，我踩过的坑告诉你"

Examples:
- "千万别这样面试，我被拒了8次才明白"
- "千万别买这些护肤品，白花了2000块"

Your formula:
```
Hook: 千万别___，我___才发现
Body: Share 3 mistakes and what to do instead
End: 还有哪些坑？评论区告诉我
```

**Write Note 3: The Free Resource Hook (5 minutes)**

Format: "免费！___个___让我___"

Examples:
- "免费！5个APP让我英语从四级到雅思7分"
- "免费！3个网站让我在家也能健身"

Your structure:
```
Hook: 免费！___个___让我___
Body: List each resource with specific benefit
End: 收藏慢慢用，顺手点个赞
```

<Checkpoint>
Do your 3 note hooks make you want to click and read more?
<Answer>If each hook includes a specific number, timeframe, or surprising result that makes you curious, they're working</Answer>
</Checkpoint>

## Step 3: Create Covers and Publish (35–50 min)

**Design Simple Covers (10 minutes)**

Use Xiaohongshu's built-in editor or any photo app:
- **Background**: Solid color (white, pink, or light blue work best)
- **Text**: Your hook in 2-3 lines, large font
- **You**: Include your photo if comfortable, or simple graphics

Template layout:
```
[Large number like "15斤" or "3个月"]
[Main benefit in smaller text]
[Your photo in corner or simple icon]
```

**Publish All 3 Notes (5 minutes)**

For each note:
1. Add your cover image
2. Paste your written content
3. Add 8-10 relevant tags (check trending tags in your niche)
4. Set location to a major city in your area
5. Publish immediately

**Timing tip**: Post your first note now, second note in 2 hours, third note tomorrow morning (8-9 AM gets best reach).

## Step 4: Kickstart Engagement (50–58 min)

**Find Your Tribe (8 minutes)**

Search your niche keywords and engage with 20 accounts:
- Like their latest 2 posts
- Leave thoughtful comments (not just "好棒!" but "我也在试这个方法，第几天最难坚持？")
- Follow accounts with similar audience size (under 5000 followers)

**Engagement Script Templates**:
```
On weight loss posts: "请问这个食谱一天大概多少卡路里呀？"
On career posts: "我也在准备转行，可以问一下学了多久吗？"
On study posts: "这个APP免费版够用吗？"
```

<Checkpoint>
Have you genuinely engaged with 20 posts in your niche area?
<Answer>You should have 20 new comment notifications and have followed accounts that post content similar to what you want to create</Answer>
</Checkpoint>

## Step 5: Ship It (55–60 min)

**Plan Your Next 7 Posts (5 minutes)**

Write down 7 more hook ideas using these proven formats:
- "第X天___，变化太明显了"
- "___vs___，差别真的很大"  
- "花了XXX块学到的___经验"
- "___的人都在用这个方法"
- "老板不会告诉你的___真相"
- "___踩雷清单，别再花冤枉钱"
- "从X到Y，我的___全记录"

🎉 **You're Live!** Your Xiaohongshu account is now positioned to attract followers who genuinely want your content. Check your analytics in 24 hours—you should see at least 100+ views on your first post if you followed the hook formulas correctly.

## 🎁 Bonus

- **Cross-pollinate niches**: Combine two interests like "程序员的省钱攻略" for less competition
- **Create series content**: "转行日记Day 1, Day 2..." keeps followers coming back  
- **Use trending sounds**: Add popular background music to boost reach in the algorithm
