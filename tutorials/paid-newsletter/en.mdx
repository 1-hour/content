## 📋 Prerequisites

- A topic you can write about weekly (tech, marketing, finance, etc.)
- Email address for platform signup
- Basic writing skills (you write emails to friends)
- 30 minutes of focused writing time

## Step 1: Choose Your Platform (0–12 min)

Compare the three best platforms by signing up for free accounts and testing their editors.

**Substack** - Best for beginners
- Go to substack.com, click "Start writing"
- Create account, choose your newsletter name
- Test the editor: simple, clean, handles paywall automatically
- Pricing: Free, then 10% of revenue

**Zhubai** - Best for Asia/China market
- Visit zhubai.love, click "开始写作" 
- Create publication, test Chinese/English support
- Check payment options (supports Alipay/WeChat Pay)
- Pricing: Free tier, then 8% fee

**Lemon Squeezy** - Best for full control
- Go to lemonsqueezy.com, create account
- Set up product, integrate with your website
- More setup required but lowest fees (3.5%)
- Best if you already have audience

Pick the platform where the editor feels most comfortable to write in.

## Step 2: Configure Pricing & Paywall (12–25 min)

Set up your subscription tiers using psychology-backed pricing.

**For Substack:**
```
Monthly: $7/month
Annual: $70/year (save $14)
Founding Member: $150/year
```

Navigate to Settings > Payments, enter:
- Monthly price: $7
- Annual discount: 17%
- Enable founding member tier
- Write benefit descriptions: "Weekly deep-dives + community access"

**For Zhubai:**
```
月付: ¥29/月
年付: ¥299/年
```

Go to 设置 > 付费设置, configure WeChat Pay/Alipay.

**For Lemon Squeezy:**
Create product tiers:
```json
{
  "basic": "$5/month",
  "pro": "$12/month", 
  "annual": "$99/year"
}
```

Test the checkout flow by making a $1 test purchase to yourself.

## Step 3: Write Your First Issue (25–45 min)

Use this proven template that converts browsers into subscribers:

```markdown
Subject: The [Number] [Tools/Secrets/Mistakes] That [Specific Result] This Week

Hook (2 sentences):
"I spent 47 hours testing AI productivity tools this week. 
3 of them literally changed how I work forever."

Preview (what paid subscribers get):
• Tool #1: [Name] - saves 2 hours daily on [specific task]
• Tool #2: [Name] - automates [specific workflow] 
• Tool #3: [Name] - [specific surprising benefit]

Free teaser (give away tool #1):
[150 words explaining first tool with screenshot]

Paywall: 
"Want the other 2 tools plus my exact setup process? 
Subscribers get the full breakdown below 👇"

Premium content (subscribers only):
• Detailed breakdown of tools #2 and #3
• Step-by-step setup screenshots
• My personal workflow integration
• Bonus: 5 tools that didn't make the cut (and why)

Call to action:
"Try [main tool] this week and reply with your results. 
Next Thursday: My system for [next week's topic]"
```

Replace the bracketed sections with your specific content. Write about something you genuinely use.

## Step 4: Polish & Publish (45–55 min)

Make your newsletter visually appealing:

**Add these elements:**
- Header image or logo (use Canva free templates)
- Bullet points instead of long paragraphs
- Bold key phrases like tool names
- One screenshot per tool mentioned

**Substack formatting:**
```
# Main headline
## Subheadings for each section
**Bold** for emphasis
> Quotes for key insights
```

**Test before publishing:**
- Send yourself a preview email
- Check mobile formatting
- Verify paywall appears correctly
- Test subscribe button works

Click publish when everything looks clean.

## Step 5: Ship It (55–60 min)

Launch your newsletter to the world:

**Share on social media:**
```
Just launched my paid newsletter: [Title]

Week 1: The 3 AI tools that 10x'd my productivity
(Giving away tool #1 free, subscribe for the other 2)

Link: [your newsletter URL]
```

**Post in relevant communities:**
- Reddit (r/entrepreneur, niche subreddits)
- Twitter with relevant hashtags
- LinkedIn with professional context
- Discord/Slack communities you're in

**Email your network:**
Send personal messages to 10 friends asking for feedback, not subscriptions.

🎉 Congratulations! You now have a live paid newsletter. Your first subscriber will feel amazing.
